Body Doubling Rooms

New

AI Social Simulator

Practice real conversations with an AI before they happen — job interviews, awkward texts, conflict resolution. Get feedback, not judgment.

Task Exploder

Paste in any overwhelming task. We break it into the smallest possible steps so your brain knows exactly where to start.

Brain Dump
